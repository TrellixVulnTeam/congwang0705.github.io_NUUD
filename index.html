About me

I received the B.S. degree in automation (Supervisor: Professor Yuncan Xue) from College of Internet of Things (IOT) Engineering, Hohai University (HHU), the M.S. degree in mathematics (Supervisor: Associate Professor Jianbin Yang) from College of Science, Hohai University (HHU), and the Ph.D. degree in mechatronic engineering (Supervisor: Professors Mengchu Zhou and Zhiwu Li) from School of Mechano-Electronic Engineering, Xidian University (XDU) in 2014, 2017, and 2021, respectively. I was a Visiting Ph.D. Student (Co-advisor: Professor Witold Pedrycz) in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Alberta, Edmonton, AB, Canada. I was also a Research Assistant (Co-advisor: Assistant Professor Jun Zhao) at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. I was also a Visiting Ph.D. Student (Co-advisor: Professor Shuzhi Sam Ge) in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, National University of Singapore, Singapore. I am currently a postdoctoral fellow under the supervision of Professor Xuelong li. My research interests include fuzzy theory, wavelet analysis, pattern recognition, granular computing, image processing, and computer vision. I am a member of China Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (CSIAM).
